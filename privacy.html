01   Introduction

Introduction

By using ExoraWorld, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. This includes data collection, processing, sharing with partners and advertising networks, and use for platform and technology improvement. If you do not agree, please do not use ExoraWorld.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of ExoraWorld — including visitors, customers, and anyone who interacts with our platform in any capacity. It explains:

  • What personal information we collect and why
  • How that information is used, stored, and protected
  • With whom it may be shared, including advertising and commercial partners
  • Your rights and choices regarding your data
  • Our liability limitations with respect to third-party data use

Your continued use of ExoraWorld — including browsing any page, downloading content, or making a purchase — constitutes your ongoing, affirmative consent to all practices described in this policy. This consent is binding from the moment of your first interaction with the platform.

03   How We Collect Data

How We Collect Data

We collect data through the following means:

  • Directly from you: When you place an order, contact us, or submit payment proof, you provide us with personal information directly
  • Automatically: When you browse ExoraWorld, we automatically collect technical and usage data through cookies, web analytics tools, and server logs
  • From payment submissions: When you submit payment proof (screenshots), we receive the information contained in that proof including transaction IDs and sender details
  • From communications: When you contact us via email, Facebook, or other channels, we collect the content of those communications
  • From third-party tools: We may use analytics services (such as Cloudflare analytics) that collect aggregated and anonymized usage data about platform performance and visitor behavior
04   How We Use Data

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: To process your order, verify payment, and deliver your purchased product to your email or phone number
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, resolve disputes, and assist with any issues related to your orders
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent payment submissions, chargebacks, or abuse of the platform
  • Communications: To send you order confirmations, delivery notifications, and important service updates related to your purchases
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze how users interact with ExoraWorld so we can improve the design, content, navigation, and overall experience
  • Security: To monitor for suspicious activity, protect the integrity of the platform, and maintain safety for all users
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, respond to legal requests, and enforce our Terms and Conditions

We do not use your data for automated decision-making that significantly affects you without human review.

05   Platform & AI Improvement

How We Use Data to Improve ExoraWorld & Our Technology

We collect and use data specifically to improve our platform, technology, artificial intelligence systems, and commercial services. This includes:

  • Traffic Analysis: Tracking which pages are visited most, session duration, and traffic sources to understand what content is most valuable to our users
  • Device Compatibility: Collecting device and browser data to identify and fix display or performance issues across different environments
  • Error Monitoring: Logging technical errors and broken interactions so our team can identify and resolve bugs promptly
  • AI & Recommendation Systems: Using behavioral and interaction data to train, improve, and operate recommendation engines, content classification systems, and AI-driven features within ExoraWorld
  • Gallery & Content Engagement: Tracking which photos and videos are viewed and downloaded most to improve content curation and user experience
  • A/B Testing: Testing different layouts, content, or designs with different user groups to identify what provides the best experience
  • Advertising Optimization: Using behavioral data to improve the relevance, performance, and placement of advertisements displayed on the platform
  • Product & Service Development: Using aggregated usage insights to guide decisions about new features, products, and services offered by ExoraWorld

Data used for platform and AI improvement may be shared with technology partners in aggregated or pseudonymized form. Individual user data may be used in non-identifiable form for machine learning and system training purposes.

10   Data Retention

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Order & Payment Records: Retained for up to 2 years for accounting, fraud prevention, and legal compliance
  • Customer Communications: Retained for up to 1 year from the date of the last interaction
  • Analytics & Usage Data: Retained in aggregated or anonymized form for up to 12 months
  • Payment Proof Screenshots: Retained for up to 90 days after order completion, then deleted unless required for an active dispute
  • Technical Logs: Server and error logs retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes

You may request early deletion of your data at any time. See Your Rights section for details.

11   Data Security

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our platform is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Access Controls: Only authorized team members can access customer order data and payment proofs
  • Cloudflare Protection: Our platform is served through Cloudflare, which provides DDoS protection, firewall rules, and threat monitoring
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive data is stored in access-controlled environments and is not kept in publicly accessible systems
  • Regular Review: We periodically review our data handling practices to ensure continued compliance with this policy

Important: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you suspect your data has been compromised, contact us immediately.

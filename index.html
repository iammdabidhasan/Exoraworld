Help Center

We've got you.

How do I pay with bKash or Nagad?
+
Click "Buy Now" on any product, enter your details, then choose bKash or Nagad. You'll see the number and exact instructions. Send the payment, enter your Transaction ID and submit. We'll verify and deliver within 1–3 hours.
How are digital products delivered?
+
After we verify your payment, the product files are sent directly to your email address. Usually within 1–3 hours, often much faster. Make sure to enter a valid email when purchasing.
Can I get a refund?
+
If you have an issue with your purchase, reach out within 7 days and we'll make it right. We stand behind every product we sell. Contact Abid directly.
How do I book a private consultation?
+
Use the contact form below or message directly on Facebook. Describe what you need, pick a time and Abid will confirm. Sessions are $20/hr or ৳2,200/hr.
Do you accept international payments?
+
Yes! International customers can pay via Airtm. We're working on adding more international options. Prices shown in both USD and BDT for clarity.
How long do service projects take?
+
It depends on the scope. Simple designs: 1–3 days. Full branding: 5–7 days. Web development: 1–3 weeks. Rush delivery is available for most projects.
